LM386 Audio Amplifier PCB
KiCad, Soldering, Testing

Took an audio amplifier from breadboard to manufactured PCB. Captured the schematic in KiCad, selected footprints, routed traces with ground planes, and iterated on track widths for manufacturability. Soldered and tested the board, measuring gain and debugging noise issues with a scope and multimeter.

Research paper

Investigated methods to detect and identify end-of-life satellites and orbital debris, including ground-based radar, optical tracking, and in situ sensing approaches. Focused on limitations in current detection pipelines and how improved sensing could support safer active debris removal missions.

Sustainable Solutions to Reduce Aviation Pollution
Team research proposal — Engineering 100W

Co-authored a comprehensive proposal analyzing how the aviation industry can reduce emissions and environmental impact using three major strategies: sustainable aviation fuels (SAFs), electric vertical takeoff and landing aircraft (eVTOL), and electric propulsion systems. The project evaluates technology, cost-benefit tradeoffs, policy frameworks, and realistic timelines for adoption, presenting a multi-stage transition plan for cleaner aviation.
