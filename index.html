I am a Computer Engineering student at San José State University with a strong interest in robotics and intelligent systems. My academic foundation includes digital logic and verification, computer architecture, embedded systems, and software engineering. I enjoy building systems where I can clearly prove they work, whether through structured testbenches, waveform validation, detailed logs, or real hardware behavior.

Currently, I am experimenting with IoT devices and machine learning, exploring how connected systems can sense, adapt, and make decisions in real time. Robotics excites me because it combines sensing, control systems, embedded software, and physical interaction with the real world into one cohesive discipline.

I care deeply about building technology with purpose. Engineering should be ethical, thoughtful, and genuinely useful. I want to contribute to systems that solve meaningful problems and improve how people interact with technology.

Outside of engineering, I am competitive in League of Legends at the Platinum level. I enjoy drawing, going to the gym, and spending time at local farmers markets. I am also a dedicated One Piece Trading Card Game player. I appreciate strategy, thoughtful design, and well built systems, whether in hardware, software, or a deck list.
